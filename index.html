Program 001 — Debut Release

The G90 M5, redrawn.

A full aerodynamic rework for BMW's new super-sedan. six components engineered as one silhouette, not six afterthoughts.

01 — Front Lip

Splitter Blade

Lowers the visual center of gravity and extends the factory bumper into a blade-like edge.

02 — Front Canards

Dive Planes

A staggered three-fin cluster that sharpens the front fascia into a more aggressive, layered profile.

03 — Side Skirts

Stance Extension

Closes the gap between the wheel arches, carrying the front splitter's line to the rear.

04 — Rear Diffuser

Quad-Fin Diffuser

Engineered around the M5's quad-exit exhaust to manage rear airflow.

05 — Rear Canards

Canard Winglets

Mirrors the front fin cluster at the rear, finished with a contrast accent for a more aggressive stance.

06 — Trunk Spoiler

Low-Profile Ducktail

Extends the roofline without disrupting the M5's proportions. OEM+ mounted.
